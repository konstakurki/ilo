Timeline of Technology

Stone tools date three million years back in time, while agriculture was invented some 12,000 years ago. Stone tools are 250 times older than farming, and farming is 250 times older than pocket calculator, which was introduced in 1970.

Because it’s difficult to grasp accelerating development like this, I made a list of 169 important inventions from the rise of agriculture to this day. If this was a traditional book, made readable by the printing press technology of the fifteenth century, it wouldn’t be practical to print the list in scale. But this is a digital book, rendered visible by computer-age inventions, which means I can do it, with virtually no cost.

So every line represents one year, and there are a lot of empty lines between the inventions. The list is long, so please scroll down fast. You can skip it, yet I recommend skimming it through. Here it comes.
